URBAN JUNGLE

THE ZOOLOGY OF SALESMEN

And how to make the most of your time with them
THE OWNER
DISTINGUISHING CHARACTERISTIC

uses "i"rather than "we"

RULES OF ENGAGEMENT

since he probably selected the stores inventory ask him why he thinks your prospective purchase is worth the money.If he cant sell you on it,its probably not worth buying

THE LIFER
DISTINGUISHING CHARACTERISTIC

can tell your size just by looking at you

RULES OF ENGAGEMENT

Having sold clothes for years,he has the wisdom and more often than not,condescending attitude to show for it.Ask informed questions to prove you are a serious shopper

THE AFTER SCHOOLER
DISTINGUISHING CHARACTERISTIC

iphone vibrating in his pocket(we hope its an iphone)

RULES OF ENGAGEMENT

He almost certainly doesnt know what hes talking about so take advantage of his energy and use him to track down sizes and ferry things to and from your fitting room.

THE RETIREE
DISTINGUISHING CHARACTERISTIC

within five minutes of meeting he will mention his former occupation

RULES OF ENGAGEMENT

He probably works part-time and may or may not know what he is talking about take whatever he says with a pinch of salt until he gives you reason not .
